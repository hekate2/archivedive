About ArchiveDive

ArchiveDive is a search engine specifically devoted to searching the old web, made for people to explore and discover content from the dawn of the internet! Webpages are mostly from platforms like GeoCities, Angelfire, and Lycos - once vibrant communities that played a major role in shaping the internet's early culture.

If you're interested in how ArchiveDive works or want to contribute to its development, you can view the full source code in the GitHub repository.
